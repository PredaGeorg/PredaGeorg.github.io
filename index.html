George Fearnley

Welcome to my webpage! I recently graduated from Durham University with a master's degree in Physics. I am currently working as an online tutor for MyTutor while looking for graduate roles in technology. Specifically, roles in software engineering or data science.

Education

After studying Maths, Physics and Chemistry at school I went on to study Physics at Durham University. I loved my time there. The people were great, the city is beautiful, and course was interesting.

I particularly enjoyed the first year 'Introduction to Programming' module, where the final project was to build a programme that traced the path of light rays initialized at random locations were traced through media of different shapes and different refractive indices. After that point, at every opportunity I opted for modules that had a programming element.

Recently I have been using cousera to teach myself web development. In fact, I have used what I learned to build this website. Please click on the more info button to see a more in depth breakdown of my education history.

Experience

I am currently working as an online tutor for MyTutor and to date I have taught over 250 classes (including some private tuition).

After graduating in 2019 I spent 7 months in Bolivia working for the Bolivian Express. I wrote articles on topics in science and gastronomy, and improving my spanish skills.

In the summer of 2018 I was awarded a summer research position within Simulations Of Soft Matter Group, lead by Dr Halim Kusumaatmaja, where I gained experience with MATLAB and bash scripting. Earlier that summer I also completed at teaching internship at Heworth Grange school in Gateshead.
